HPE Brick Game

Human pose estimation based brick game

The game is played with the help of human pose predicted by PoseNet. Please allow the website to access your camera and in the live feed the output for estimated pose is diplayed.

Steps to successfully play the game

  • Allow camera access
  • Make sure you are visible in fromt of the camera
  • Tilt yourself to left/right see the paddle moving
  • Press play button once you are ready
  • Enjoy and beat the game!!!
